Calorie Calculator

This Python program calculates the total calories burned from walking and running activities.

How to Use

Run the program.

Enter the minutes of walking and calories burned per minute separated by a space.
Example:

30 5


This means 30 minutes of walking with 5 calories burned per minute.

Enter the minutes of running and calories burned per minute separated by a space.
Example:

20 10


The program will calculate and display the total calories burned.

Example
Input:
30 5
20 10

Output:
350
